# Project Goal

# Jewelry Business Intelligence System using ERP Data

## Introduction

The jewelry industry generates a significant amount of business data every day through sales transactions, inventory updates, customer purchases, and product management. While Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software efficiently manages these day-to-day operations, it often focuses on recording transactions rather than providing meaningful business insights for strategic decision-making.

This project aims to bridge that gap by developing a **Jewelry Business Intelligence System** that transforms raw ERP data into actionable insights using interactive dashboards, analytics, and intelligent recommendations.

The system utilizes ERP-generated data (dummy datasets for academic purposes) and processes it to provide comprehensive business reports, visualizations, and predictive analysis that assist business owners and managers in making informed decisions.

---

# Project Goal

The primary goal of this project is to design and develop a **Business Intelligence Dashboard** for jewelry businesses that analyzes ERP data to improve business performance through data-driven decision making.

Instead of replacing existing ERP software, this system acts as an intelligent analytical layer that extracts valuable insights from business data and presents them through interactive dashboards and reports.

The system enables management to monitor business performance, identify sales trends, optimize inventory levels, understand customer behavior, and forecast future business requirements.

---

# Primary Objectives

## 1. Sales Analytics

Analyze sales transactions to generate meaningful business insights.

Features include:

* Daily Sales Analysis
* Monthly Revenue Trends
* Yearly Performance Comparison
* Best Selling Products
* Category-wise Sales Analysis
* Average Order Value
* Revenue Growth Analysis

---

## 2. Inventory Intelligence

Monitor and analyze inventory performance using business metrics.

The system provides:

* Current Stock Overview
* Inventory Valuation
* Low Stock Alerts
* Overstock Detection
* Dead Stock Identification
* Category-wise Inventory Distribution
* Product Movement Analysis

---

## 3. Customer Analytics

Understand customer purchasing behavior to improve customer relationship management.

The dashboard includes:

* Customer Segmentation
* Premium Customer Identification
* Repeat Purchase Analysis
* Customer Lifetime Value
* Purchase Frequency Analysis
* Geographic Distribution of Customers

---

## 4. Business Performance Dashboard

Provide management with real-time Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).

The dashboard displays:

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Total Customers
* Inventory Value
* Gross Profit
* Monthly Growth Percentage
* Fast Moving Products
* Slow Moving Products

---

## 5. Demand Prediction

Analyze historical sales data to estimate future demand.

The prediction module helps management by:

* Forecasting Monthly Sales
* Estimating Product Demand
* Identifying Seasonal Trends
* Supporting Inventory Planning

Initially, the system uses statistical methods such as Moving Average and Growth Rate Analysis, making the implementation suitable for a BCA-level project while leaving room for future machine learning enhancements.

---

## 6. Dead Stock Detection

Identify products that have not been sold for a specified period (e.g., 90 days).

Benefits include:

* Reduced Inventory Holding Cost
* Better Warehouse Utilization
* Improved Cash Flow
* Identification of Slow-Moving Products

---

## 7. Restock Recommendation Engine

Provide intelligent recommendations for inventory replenishment based on historical sales patterns.

The recommendation considers:

* Current Stock Quantity
* Average Monthly Sales
* Product Demand
* Safety Stock Level

This assists business owners in maintaining optimal inventory levels while minimizing stock shortages.

---

## 8. Data Visualization

Convert raw business data into easy-to-understand visual reports.

The system includes:

* Bar Charts
* Line Charts
* Pie Charts
* Area Charts
* KPI Cards
* Trend Analysis
* Interactive Dashboards

Visualization enables management to quickly interpret business performance without manually analyzing spreadsheets.

# Scope of the Project

This project focuses on developing an analytical dashboard using ERP-generated data. The system does not perform billing or inventory transactions directly but processes exported business data to generate reports, analytics, and predictions.

The application includes modules for:

* Sales Analytics
* Inventory Analytics
* Customer Analytics
* Business Dashboard
* Demand Prediction
* Dead Stock Detection
* Restock Recommendations
* Report Generation
